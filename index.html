Passive High-Pass Filter

Components used: Resistor, Capacitor, Power supply, Oscilloscope

Virtually Represented with: NI Multisim

A passive high-pass filter circuit is composed of resistors and capacitors. The capacitor's reactance decreases with increasing frequency, allowing higher frequency signals to pass through. The resistor and capacitor together set the cutoff frequency, enabling the filtration of lower frequencies.
